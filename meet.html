An international sweetheart, Eggs can be found in your favorite dessert or baked goods. But don’t be fooled by her sweet tastes, Eggs also has a savory side. Catch her in your next brunch entree—she’s a guaranteed headliner.
⚡   Warning: most severe of all the allergies ⚡
Something smells fishy... must be Seafood! He’s the prankster of the group and can take many forms, including sushi, lobster, oysters, salmon, and many more. A good rule of thumb: if it comes from the ocean (and it’s not seaweed), it’s a no-go.
⚡   Warning: very severe allergy ⚡
With veganism and healthy eating blowing up in the Bay Area, most people are nuts about Nuts! Included in Avery’s Allergies are most tree nuts (cashews, almonds, pecans, walnuts, pistachios, etc) but don’t include peanuts (which are a legume, not a nut, if you wanna get technical).
🥜 Peanuts, pine nuts, coconuts, and macadamia nuts are not included as an allergy, which means they’re fine for Avery to eat!
Yeah, we’re not really sure if he’s a real Allergy, or just an Intolerance. He was one of the last Allergies to emerge, and usually shows himself in undercooked beef, and is especially bad in steak and burgers.
🐄  Assess on a case by case basis, but usually it's no.
It’s unclear whether the Banana is an Allergy or just a really intense dislike, but either way, he’s part of the group to stay.

Kiwi is oh-so-sweet, but she packs a real allergic punch, along with her Chinese cousin, Dragonfruit. Fun fact: last consumed in 2008!
